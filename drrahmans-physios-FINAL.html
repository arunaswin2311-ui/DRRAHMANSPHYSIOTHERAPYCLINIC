Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit with us.

Do I need a doctor's referral to visit? ＋
No referral is needed. You can book a direct appointment with Dr. Ijaj Rahman. However, if you have recent scans or reports, please bring them along for a more accurate assessment.
What should I bring for my first visit? ＋
Bring any relevant medical reports, X-rays, or MRI scans, and wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing to allow easy examination of the affected area.
How many sessions will I need? ＋
The number of sessions depends on your condition and severity. Many patients feel significant relief within 3–5 sessions. Dr. Ijaj Rahman will create a personalised plan after your initial assessment.
Do you offer home visit physiotherapy? ＋
Yes! Home visits are available for patients who are unable to travel to the clinic. Please call or WhatsApp us on 6382927750 to arrange a home visit appointment.
What conditions do you treat? ＋
We treat neurological (stroke, Parkinson's, vertigo, spinal cord), orthopaedic (back pain, disc bulge, cervical spondylosis, knee pain), and sports injuries (sprains, strains, ligament tears). Dr. Ijaj Rahman handles every domain.
What treatments and equipment are available? ＋
We offer IFT (Interferential Therapy), TENS, Wax Therapy, Ultrasound Therapy, Manual Therapy, therapeutic exercises and more — all using modern, well-maintained equipment at our Villivakkam clinic.
